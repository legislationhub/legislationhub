    Draft SIs

    Before the SIs are formally 'laid' in Parliament, they have to go through a sifting process. Draft EU Exit legislation sets out the proposed amendments to UK legislation and amendments to retained EU law.

    Statutory Instruments

    The UK Government will publish around 1000 statutory instruments and the purpose of the amendments is to ensure the UK’s laws work properly when the UK leaves the EU.

    EU Retained Law

    As the UK leaves the EU, it is converting most of EU law into a new type of domestic law. "Retained EU law" is created by the EU (Withdrawal) Act 2018 and will come into effect on "exit day" and will replicate several different sources of EU law as domestic equivalents.
